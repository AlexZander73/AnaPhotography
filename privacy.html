Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information may be collected through this website, how it is used, and how booking-related information is handled.

1. Information Collected

When you submit a booking or contact enquiry, information you choose to provide may include your name, email address, social handle, service type, preferred dates, location details, creative preferences, and any other information relevant to your booking.

2. How Information Is Collected

This website currently uses an embedded Google Form for booking requests. When you submit information through that form, your data is processed and stored through Google’s services according to Google’s own terms and privacy practices.

3. How Information Is Used

Information submitted through the website may be used to respond to enquiries, confirm availability, prepare quotes, manage bookings, communicate before and after shoots, and deliver photography services.

4. Sharing of Information

Personal information is not sold. Information may be shared only where reasonably necessary for business operations, such as payment processing, gallery delivery, scheduling, subcontracted production support, legal compliance, or platform hosting.

5. Data Retention

Enquiry and booking information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to enquiries, manage projects, comply with legal obligations, maintain business records, and resolve disputes or follow-up requests.

6. Embedded Services

Because the booking form is embedded from Google Forms, your browser may load content directly from Google. That may include cookies, technical logs, or related browser metadata controlled by Google rather than this website.

7. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to use reputable tools and keep access to enquiry data limited. However, no website, form system, email system, or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit the form. If you have already submitted personal information and want to request correction or deletion where appropriate, you may contact the business directly.

9. Third-Party Links

This website may link to external platforms such as Instagram, Google Forms, or other third-party services. Those services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

10. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect operational, technical, or legal changes. The latest published version on the website applies to current website use.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, use the contact options provided on the website.
